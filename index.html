What's TravelUrr?

TravelUrr allows you to let your friends and family follow you on your travels around the world. Let them enjoy the warm beaches of the southern hemisphere, or shiver right along with you on snowy mountain slopes. Share your location and let them join you on your journey!



Sounds awesome! how does it work?

It's simple really! Just download the TravelUrr app to your smartphone. You will automatically be registered at TravelUrr.com. Next you'll need to add a bunch of friends and family. Now all you need is an awesome holiday destination! From there you can use the app to upload your holiday photo's daily. Not the photogenic type? No problem! Use it as a blog to share your thoughts, words of wisdom or even epiphanies.. Everybody with a link can read your most inner thoughts and most tantalising imagery, whilest being protected from random browsers. Let your travelUrr-companions register as well so they can write comments and witty though safety-conscious remarks ( we all know how our moms are;) ). So sign up today and become a TravelUrr!
